# Reliable RPC Service

[**RPC Edge**](https://thirdweb.com/rpc-edge) is a reliable RPC service for EVM chains, offering **low latency** via geographically distributed servers, **smart routing**, and **caching RPC responses**.  

RPC Edge’s endpoints cover **150+ worldwide locations** and provide maintained uptime and high reliability with affordable rates — even at scale.

[Learn more about RPC Edge in the technical docs](https://portal.thirdweb.com/infrastructure/rpc-edge/overview)
